Put butter and milk in a saucepan and heat gently until butter has melted. Stir in the vanilla essence
Sift flour, sugar, and cocoa into a bowl
Beat in the milk mixture
Pour into greased ovenproof dish
Combine the topping ingredients and sprinkle over mixture
Carefully pour the hot water over it
Put into oven at 180\textcelsius{} for about 45 minutes
