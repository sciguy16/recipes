Combine the brown and the granulated sugars in a large bowl, breaking up any large chunks
Whisk the butter into the sugar mixture until the mixture starts to pull away from the sides of the bowl
Whisk in the eggs and vanilla extract
Add the flour, salt, and baking powder and mix well
Mix in the chocolate chips
Dollop onto baking trays, allowing plenty of room for them to expand and bake at 170\textcelsius{} for 10-14 minutes until the edges are set and the cookies are beginning to turn golden brown
Leave in place to cool
