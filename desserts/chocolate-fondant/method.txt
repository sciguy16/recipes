Gently melt the butter and chocolate together
Whisk the eggs, sugar, and salt until light and pale
Fold in the chocolate mixture a little at a time
Fold in the flour
Divide into 6 sections of a greased muffin tray (or cake cases)
Bake at 180\textcelsius{} for 7-8 minutes. Cakes should be firm on the top but squidgy in the centre
Rest for 3 minutes before turning out onto a plate and serving with ice cream
