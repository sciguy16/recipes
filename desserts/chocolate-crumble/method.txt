Mix the cornflour, cocoa, and orange rind to a paste with a little of the milk.
Bring the remaining milk and juice to the boil in a saucepan.
Pour a little into the cornflour mixture, stir in and gradually blend all the liquid and paste together. Return to saucepan, heat, and stir until thickened.
Stir in sugar
Transfer to ovenproof dish
Melt butter in saucepan, then add oats and mix thoroughly
Remove from heat and stir in sugar to make the crumble.
Put crumble on top of mixture in the dish and bake at 180\textcelsius{} for 10-15 minutes until the top is crisp
