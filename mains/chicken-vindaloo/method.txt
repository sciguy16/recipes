Grind cumin, black pepper, cardamom, cinnamon, mustard, and fenugreek together.
Combine with vinegar, salt, cayenne pepper, and brown sugar and set aside.
Fry the onions until they are a rich, dark brown.
Puree the onions and combine with 3 tbsp water and the spice mixture into a smooth paste. This is the vindaloo paste.
Blend the garlic and ginger with 1 tbsp water into a smooth paste.
Heat the oil in a large pan and add the garlic-ginger paste. Heat until the paste browns slightly, then add the coriander and turmeric.
Add the chicken, and brown lightly.
Add the vindaloo paste, passata, peppers, and chillies and bring to the boil.
Reduce the heat annd simmer for 45-60 minutes, or until the chicken has cooked and the sauce thickened slightly.
Serve with rice.
