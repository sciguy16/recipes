Combine yoghurt, 2 tsp garam masala, 2 tsp paprika, $\frac12$ tsp black pepper, $\frac12$ tsp salt, $\frac12$ tsp cayenne pepper, and $\frac12$ tsp coriander in a large bowl. Add chicken strips and toss to coat. Cover and marinate in the fridge for 2 hours.
Preheat oven to 230\textcelsius{} and grease a baking tray
Place chicken strips on tray and bake until no longer pink inside; about 10 minutes. (alternatively, marinate the chicken in an oven proof dish and transfer straight from fridge to oven)
Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds and stir until lightly toasted and aromatic. Add onion and stir until it begins to soften. Stir in garlic, 1 tbs ginger, and green chillies and continue to cook until onion is browned. Add tomatoes, tomato puree, and water into the onion mixture and cook until tomatoes begin to break down.
Add 1 tsp garam masala, $\frac12$ tsp coriander, and $\frac12$ tsp turmeric into tomato mixture. Mix in the cooked chicken, add cream, and stir to coat. Cover and let simmer for 10 minutes.
