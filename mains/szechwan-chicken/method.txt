Whisk the soy sauce, sherry, sugar, ginger, cornstarch, crushed red pepper, and garlic in a large bowl
Add the chicken, ensuring it's coated fully, then cover and marinate in the fridge for 1 to 2 hours
Fry the onion and pepper for a couple of minutes, then add the marinated chicken (including marinade) and cook until the chicken is mostly cooked
Add the cashews and cook for another couple of minutes, then serve with rice
