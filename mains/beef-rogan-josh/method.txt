Add garam masala, chilli powder, turmeric, paprika, allspice, cumin, cinnamon, and pepper to a large saucepan and gently bring to a high heat until very aromatic, taking care not to burn the spices
Add the butter and oil and heat until smoking, followed by the onions and stir fry for about 5 minutes
Add the garlic and ginger, fry for about a minute, then add the beef
Once the beef has browned, add the tomato quarters and the lime juice and cook for a further 5 minutes
Add the tinned tomatoes and the beef stock and bring to the boil
Reduce the heat and simmer for about 1 hour 20, until the beef is tender and the sauce is thick, making sure to stir periodically to prevent sticking or burning
Add the coriander 10 minutes before the end of cooking
Leave to stand for about 10 minutes, then servce with rice and naan bread
